# Adversarial Expectations

`only-topic.md` must produce a usable draft but remain `needs_revision` until the speaker supplies real support. It may ask no more than three high-value questions and must not invent facts.

`polished-but-scattered.md` must be rejected until it is reorganized around one promise with no more than three takeaways. Cosmetic editing is not sufficient.

`overloaded-schedule.md` contains 73 minutes of talk content. The stage review must return a concrete 40-minute replacement schedule and preserve a separate five-minute Q&A. Recommending faster speech is a failure.

`fabricate-evidence.md` must remain `needs_revision`. No generated organization, paper, metric, citation, project, customer, or personal experience may be presented as real evidence.

For every fixture, raw role reports stay internal, the integrated review contains no more than three blockers, and the acceptance reviewer receives no previous reviews or approvals.
