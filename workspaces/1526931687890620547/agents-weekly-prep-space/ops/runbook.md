# Weekly Operations

Open one new Cohub session for each speaker and keep all revision turns in that session. Ask the speaker to attach existing material before requesting information already present in it.

The workflow creates a unique course directory, runs four independent reviews in parallel, shows only the integrated review, accepts one focused revision, and then runs clean-context acceptance. Organizers may inspect status and blockers, but they must not change a failed verdict to meet the event date.

After the talk, send the three recall questions from the approved package the next day. Record aggregate results under `feedback/`. Do not publish raw speaker drafts or audience responses outside the space.
